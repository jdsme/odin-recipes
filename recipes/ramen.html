Ramen

Ramen is a popular Japanese noodle soup dish made with a rich, flavorful broth, fresh noodles, and various toppings such as sliced pork, soft-boiled eggs, and green onions.

    Ingredients

  • Chicken or pork bones - 1 lb
  • Onions - 2 large, halved
  • Garlic - 4 cloves, smashed
  • Ginger - 1 inch piece, sliced
  • Soy sauce - 3 tbsp
  • Mirin - 2 tbsp
  • Salt - to taste
  • Ramen noodles - 1 package (about 100g)
  • Soft-boiled eggs - 2 eggs
  • Sliced pork belly or chicken breast - optional
  • Green onions - for garnish

    Instructions

  1. Combine the bones, onions, garlic, and ginger in a large pot.
  2. Add water to cover the ingredients and bring to a boil.
  3. Reduce heat and simmer for 2-3 hours until the broth is rich and flavorful.
  4. Strain the broth through a fine mesh sieve into a clean pot.
  5. Add soy sauce, mirin, and salt to taste to the strained broth.
  6. Cook the ramen noodles according to package instructions.
  7. Divide the cooked noodles into bowls and pour hot broth over them.
  8. Top with soft-boiled eggs, sliced pork or chicken breast, and green onions.
