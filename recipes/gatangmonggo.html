Ginataang Monggo

Description

Ginataang Monggo is a traditional Filipino dessert made from mung beans cooked in coconut milk and sweetened with sugar. It's a creamy and comforting dish often enjoyed as a snack or dessert.

    Ingredients

  • Mung beans - 1 cup, soaked and drained
  • Coconut milk - 1 can (14 oz)
  • Sugar - 1/2 cup (adjust to taste)
  • Water - 1/2 cup

    Instructions

  1. In a large pot, combine the soaked mung beans, coconut milk, and water.
  2. Bring the mixture to a boil over high heat.
  3. Reduce heat to low and simmer for about 30-40 minutes until the beans are tender and the mixture is thickened.
  4. Add sugar and stir until dissolved.
  5. Continue cooking for another 5-10 minutes until the dish reaches desired consistency.
  6. Remove from heat and let it cool slightly before serving.
