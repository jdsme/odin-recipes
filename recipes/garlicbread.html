Garlic Bread

Description

Garlic bread is a delicious side dish made from bread topped with garlic, butter, and herbs, then toasted to perfection.

It's a popular accompaniment to Italian meals like pasta and pizza, known for its crispy texture and rich, savory flavor.

    Ingredients

  • Baguette or Italian bread - 1 loaf
  • Unsalted butter - 1/2 cup (1 stick), softened
  • Garlic - 4-5 cloves, minced
  • Fresh parsley - 2 tablespoons, chopped (optional)
  • Salt - 1/2 teaspoon
  • Grated Parmesan cheese - 1/4 cup (optional)

    Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Slice the baguette in half lengthwise.
  3. In a bowl, mix the softened butter, minced garlic, chopped parsley, and salt until well combined.
  4. Spread the garlic butter mixture evenly over the cut sides of the bread.
  5. If desired, sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese on top of the buttered bread.
  6. Place the bread halves on a baking sheet, cut side up.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for about 10-15 minutes, or until the bread is golden and crispy around the edges.
  8. For extra crispiness, you can broil the bread for an additional 1-2 minutes, but watch closely to prevent burning.
  9. Remove from the oven, let it cool slightly, then slice and serve warm.
